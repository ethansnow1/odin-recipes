Lasagna

worlds-best-lasagna

This is the worlds most AMAZING lasagna recipe that has been passed down in my family for generations.

We have been making this recipe since before the even the conventional oven had been created, we used to sit this outside our caves and whatnot to bake underneath the brutal sun. We would have to occasionally have to fight off dinosaurs and other beasts from it, its just that good!

Ingredients

    The most luxurious of wagyu hamburger meat
    Wooly mammoth cheese
    The most extra extra extra olive oil
    Lasagna noodles, I don't know, get them from Walmart

Steps

  1. First, brown your wagyu beef
  2. Then, add in some seasoning (whatever you got lying around
  3. Next, boil your noodles until very soft
  4. Finally, layer everything in a dish and use absolutely every last piece of cheese to cover top
  5. Bake at 500 degrees until cheese is golden brown
  • Now enjoy the worlds best lasagna!
